Week 8 – Testing & Evaluation – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 8

1. Imagine you are making a version 2.0 of your project. Describe two specific improvements you would incorporate based on your evaluation.

2. Write a short scenario of a user interacting with your product. Identify any minor inconveniences and how you might refine the design.

3. Reflect on how the project gave you insight into real design and technology careers. Which skills did you practise that engineers or tradespeople use?
