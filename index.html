Course Overview

You’ll progress through the entire design process: research, ideation, planning, marking, cutting, shaping, joining, finishing, testing and reflection.

Choose a learning path below that suits your needs. Main theory covers the core lessons and quizzes, support theory offers simplified explanations and practice questions, advanced theory poses deeper, open‑ended challenges, and interactive scenarios let you test your decision‑making skills in realistic situations.
