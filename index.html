Overview

This unit guides you through the design and construction of a metal hose reel holder over ten weeks. You will learn workshop safety, design processes, marking and cutting, shaping, joining, finishing, testing and evaluation.

Select one of the learning paths below to jump to the weekly content. The main theory covers the core lessons and quizzes, the support content offers simplified explanations and practice questions, and the advanced content poses deeper, open‑ended challenges.
