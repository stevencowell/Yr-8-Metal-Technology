Week 7: Finishing Techniques

Support Theory

Finishing makes the project safe and long lasting. File off sharp edges, sand and clean the metal, then apply primer and paint. Always paint in a ventilated area wearing a mask and goggles. Proper disposal of paint and cans protects the environment.
