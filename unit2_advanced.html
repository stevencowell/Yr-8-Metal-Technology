Week 2 – Design & Planning – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 2

1. Imagine your teacher asks for an additional feature such as holding a spray nozzle. How would you revise your design to include this new requirement?

2. Suggest one way to make your design more sustainable – for example, using less material or incorporating recycled off‑cuts. Explain your idea.

3. Research how professional designers plan projects. Choose one tool (such as CAD software, a Gantt chart or checklists) and describe how it helps manage a real design project.
