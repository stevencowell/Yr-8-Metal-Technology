Week 3 – Tools & Marking Out – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 3

1. Investigate what could happen if a hole is drilled slightly off target – will the bolt still fit? How do professionals achieve high precision when drilling?

2. Research a power tool or machine that could improve marking or cutting accuracy (e.g. a guillotine shear or CNC laser cutter). Explain how it works and what safety considerations it has.

3. Explain how material properties such as hardness and toughness affect the way we mark and cut metal. Why is a hardened scribe necessary and why do drill bits heat up?
