Weekly Schedule

This program outlines the focus, activities and outcomes for each week of the Hose Reel Holder project. Teachers may adapt dates and registration details to suit their class timetable.

WeekFocusActivitiesOutcomes
1 Introduction & Safety Project brief, material selection, workshop rules, PPE, safety quiz, first sketches. TE4-1DP, TE4-3DP
2 Design & Planning Research existing holders, brainstorm ideas, plan design and production steps. TE4-1DP, TE4-2DP
3 Tools & Marking Out Learn to measure, mark and cut metal accurately using appropriate tools. TE4-3DP, TE4-9MA
4 Shaping Techniques Bend and form metal using vices, hammers and jigs. Learn about malleability and spring-back. TE4-3DP, TE4-9MA
5 Joining Techniques Join components with bolts, screws, welding or brazing. Discuss pros and cons and safety. TE4-3DP, TE4-9MA
6 Project Construction Assemble the pieces, align parts, fix minor errors and complete the structure. TE4-2DP, TE4-3DP
7 Finishing Techniques File and sand edges; apply primer and paint; discuss alternative finishes and environmental care. TE4-3DP, TE4-9MA
8 Testing & Evaluation Mount and test the holder; gather feedback; evaluate against criteria; reflect on improvements. TE4-1DP, TE4-10TS
9 Portfolio Completion Compile project documentation: brief, research, sketches, process diary, evaluation, reflection. TE4-1DP, TE4-2DP, TE4-3DP
10 Final Assessment & Reflection Present and submit final product and portfolio; complete final quiz; reflect on learning. TE4-10TS
