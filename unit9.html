Week 9 – Portfolio Completion

In the final preparation week students compile a portfolio documenting their entire project. This includes the project brief, research on existing designs, initial sketches, final drawings with dimensions, a production plan, photos or descriptions of the making process, test results and reflections. The portfolio should be organised chronologically with clear headings and captions. It demonstrates how the outcomes were met: design, planning, production, materials understanding and societal context. Good spelling, grammar and neat layout make it easy for the reader to follow.

Case Study

Alex’s portfolio is incomplete – he has a couple of sketches and a few notes but no record of how he solved problems or what he learned. Bella’s portfolio is thorough: she includes research images, multiple design sketches, a detailed plan, photos of each stage of construction, test results and a thoughtful reflection. Her portfolio clearly shows evidence for each outcome and makes it easy to assess her work.
