Week 1: Introduction & Safety

Support Theory

This week is about learning what the project is and how to stay safe. You need to wear safety glasses all the time, keep the area tidy and listen to the teacher. Mild steel is used because it’s strong and can handle the weather. You don’t start cutting until you understand the safety rules.
