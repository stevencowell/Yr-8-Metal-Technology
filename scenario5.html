Scenario

A student attempts to join two steel plates. They start welding without cleaning off oil and rust. They neglect to fit up the plates and set the welder current too low. The weld bead looks poor and doesn’t penetrate. Frustrated, they decide to switch to riveting but forget to drill pilot holes, bending the rivets.
