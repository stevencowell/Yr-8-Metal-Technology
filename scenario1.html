Scenario

During the first lesson, a student rushes into the metal workshop without waiting for the teacher’s instructions. Excited to begin, they start using a hacksaw on a piece of mild steel without putting on safety glasses or ear protection and without clamping the metal. Midway through the cut the workpiece slides, nearly injuring their hand. Tools and offcuts are scattered around their bench, creating a trip hazard. The teacher stops the activity and reminds the class of the safety briefing they had just completed.
