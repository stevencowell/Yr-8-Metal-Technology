Week 6 – Project Construction – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 6

1. Describe a specific challenge you faced during assembly and how you addressed it. What did you learn that will change how you design or build in future?

2. Explain the concept of tolerance in manufacturing. How does allowing a small amount of clearance between parts make assembly easier? Give an example.

3. Identify one aspect of your design or process that you would improve if you built the project again. Explain your reasoning.
