Week 10: Final Assessment & Reflection

Support Theory

In the final week you demonstrate your work, complete any tests and reflect on what you have learned. Be proud of your achievements and think about how the skills gained apply to future projects.

Week 10 Quiz

  1. Why is reflection important at the end of a project?




  2. What do you typically do during the final assessment?




  3. How should you feel about your achievements after completing the project?




  4. How can the skills from this project be useful in the future?




  5. Why is it important to complete any tests when presenting your project?
