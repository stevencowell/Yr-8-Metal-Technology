Week 7 – Finishing Techniques – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 7

1. Research an alternative finish such as powder coating or galvanizing. How is it applied and how does its durability compare to paint?

2. Find out what health risks are associated with inhaling paint fumes or metal dust. How do professionals mitigate these risks?

3. Propose a creative finishing idea that adds personal flair to your project (e.g. two‑tone colours or a stencil). Explain how you would apply it while still protecting the metal.
