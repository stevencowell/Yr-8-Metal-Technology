Week 9 – Portfolio Completion – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 9

1. Create a table in your portfolio where you self‑assess how you met each syllabus outcome (TE4‑1DP through TE4‑10TS). Provide brief evidence for each.

2. Write a two‑minute presentation script summarising your project for an audience. Mention the problem, your design, how you made it and what you learned.

3. Imagine how this project could lead to other work. What could you make next using these skills? Describe an extension project.
