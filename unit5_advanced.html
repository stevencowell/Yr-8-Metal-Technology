Week 5 – Joining Techniques – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 5

1. Compare the strength and suitability of welding, brazing and bolting for three different situations (e.g. a bridge, a thin sheet metal panel and a swing set). Explain why each scenario uses a specific method.

2. Describe a simple test you could perform on your joint to check its strength before putting the project into service.

3. Research a modern joining method that doesn’t involve traditional welding or bolting (e.g. high‑strength adhesives or friction stir welding). How does it work and where is it used?
