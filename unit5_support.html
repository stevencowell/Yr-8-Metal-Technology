Week 5: Joining Techniques

Support Theory

You learn different ways to join metal. Bolts and screws can be undone; welds and brazes permanently fuse the pieces. Glue is not strong enough for load‑bearing metal projects. Always wear proper protection when welding or brazing and make sure the parts fit well before joining.
