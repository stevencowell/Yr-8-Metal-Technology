Stage 4 Technology Mandatory Outcomes

This metalwork project unit aligns with the NSW Stage 4 Technology Mandatory syllabus. Key outcomes addressed include:

  • TE4‑1DP: Students design, communicate and evaluate products and processes that solve real and relevant problems.
  • TE4‑2DP: Students plan and manage the production of designed solutions, including safe use of tools and resources.
  • TE4‑3DP: Students select and safely apply a broad range of tools, materials and processes to produce quality solutions.
  • TE4‑9MA: Students analyse how characteristics and properties of materials, tools and techniques influence design and making.
  • TE4‑10TS: Students explain how people in technology related professions contribute to society now and in the future.

For the full syllabus, visit the NSW Education Standards Authority website. An official PDF can be downloaded from their Technology Mandatory page.
