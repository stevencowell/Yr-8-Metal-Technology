Advanced Activities – Week 4

1. You bent a piece but the angle is 5° off. Describe how you might correct or compensate for this error without starting over.

2. Research what happens to steel when you heat it and then let it cool. How does annealing make steel easier to bend?

3. Choose an industrial shaping machine such as a press brake or rolling machine. Explain how it works and why it produces consistent bends.
