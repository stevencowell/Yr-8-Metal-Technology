Week 10 – Final Assessment & Reflection – Advanced Theory

Complete the following open‑ended tasks. Use the text boxes below to submit your responses. When you click the submit button your answers will be sent to the teacher’s Google Sheet.

Advanced Activities – Week 10

1. Research a career related to the skills you used (e.g. boilermaker, industrial designer or mechanical engineer). What do they do and which skills from the project are relevant?

2. Suggest one improvement to the project task or how the unit was run that would help future students learn more or enjoy it more.

3. Write a short reflection on how your confidence with tools and project management changed from Week 1 to Week 10.
