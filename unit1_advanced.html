Advanced Activities – Week 1

1. Research how professional workshops enforce safety. Describe two procedures or equipment used in industry that are similar to or beyond our school rules.

2. Pick another metal (e.g. aluminium or stainless steel) and compare its properties to mild steel for making an outdoor, load‑bearing project. Would it be a better choice? Explain.

3. Think of an additional feature to add to your project (for example, a clip to secure a stored item). Sketch or describe your idea and explain how it would help.
