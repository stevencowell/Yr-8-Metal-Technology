Assessment Overview

Students will be assessed on their ability to design, plan, produce, evaluate and reflect on a functioning hose reel holder. Assessment components include:

  • Quizzes: Each unit page contains a short multiple‑choice quiz. Completion and performance contribute to formative assessment.
  • Practical Project: The finished hose holder is assessed on how well it meets the brief (function, strength, safety and finish) and demonstrates safe and skilful use of tools.
  • Portfolio: The design portfolio documents research, ideas, planning, making, testing and reflection. It should show evidence for all outcomes.
  • Reflection: Students present their projects and reflect on their learning. A short written quiz may also cover key knowledge from the unit.
